ANSWERS TO FREQUENTLY
ASKED QUESTIONS

IS THE TRAINING ONLY FOR BEGINNERS ?

No, the training is designed to cater to a diverse group of learners. While beginners will find foundational modules to get started, there are also intermediate and advanced sections for those who have some prior experience.

WHAT FORMAT WILL THE TRAINING BE IN ?

The training will be delivered in a blended learning format. This includes interactive online modules, downloadable resources, live virtual sessions with experts, and hands-on project assignments. We believe this multi-format approach caters to different learning styles and ensures comprehensive understanding.

WILL THERE BE FEEDBACK FROM ELANORA AND CURATORS ?

Absolutely! Elanora, along with other curators, will be actively involved in providing feedback. After the submission of each assignment or project, participants will receive detailed feedback to help them understand their areas of strength and where they can improve. Additionally, there will be Q&A sessions where participants can seek direct input from Elanora and other experts.

WILL I BE ABLE TO COMBINE STUDY AND WORK ?

Yes, the training is designed keeping in mind the schedules of working professionals. Most of the content is asynchronous, allowing participants to access it at their own pace and convenience. Live sessions will be scheduled in advance and will also be recorded for those who might miss them. We encourage a steady pace for optimal learning, but we also understand the demands of balancing work and study.
